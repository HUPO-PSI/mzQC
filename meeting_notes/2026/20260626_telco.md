## Lipidomics Use Case

- Franziska and Nils switched to a new lipidomics dataset after the previous one was found to be flawed. Bo already had a workflow in place, making it straightforward to export to mzQC.
- Around 3 metrics were chosen to begin with. Several CV terms are missing and need to be defined.
- A key open question is the **signal to blank ratio**: calculated as the median of samples divided by the median of process blanks, it can apply to the entire run. The group needs to decide whether to define a single general CV term (with risk of ambiguity) or specific derived terms encoding each particular calculation.
- A related structural question: is this a run level or set level metric? It depends on at least two runs (sample run + blank run), so it does not fit neatly into either category. It may apply to multiple runs in a set.
- Similarly, the **coefficient of variation** should be made specific rather than general separate terms for pooled BQCs (batch quality controls) and TQCs (technical quality controls) are recommended, since not all datasets will have both.
- General principle agreed: make metric definitions explicit and specific to the calculation being used, if someone needs a variant, they can request a related term rather than trying to make definitions overly general upfront.
